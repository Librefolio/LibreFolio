# <img src="../../../static/icons/asset-types/hold.png" width="32" style="vertical-align: middle;" /> Commodities

**Commodities** are physical assets — raw materials or primary goods — held for long-term value preservation or as an inflation hedge. In LibreFolio, this category covers precious metals, energy, and other tangible stores of value.

---

## 🔑 Key Characteristics

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Code in LibreFolio** | `HOLD` |
| **Pricing** | Spot prices from commodity exchanges, or manual valuation |
| **Currency** | Typically quoted in USD (gold, oil) but can be any currency |
| **Dividends** | None — commodities do not generate income |
| **Storage** | Physical commodities may incur storage/insurance costs |
| **Typical providers** | Yahoo Finance (e.g., `GC=F` for gold futures), Manual |

---

## 📊 Common Commodities

| Commodity | Symbol | Role in Portfolio |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| **Gold** | `GC=F` | Safe haven, inflation hedge |
| **Silver** | `SI=F` | Industrial + precious metal |
| **Oil (Crude)** | `CL=F` | Energy exposure |
| **Natural Gas** | `NG=F` | Energy exposure |

---

## 📈 Why Hold Commodities

- **Inflation hedge**: Commodity prices tend to rise with inflation
- **Diversification**: Low correlation with stocks and bonds
- **Safe haven**: Gold historically performs well during market crises
- **No counterparty risk**: Physical gold has no issuer that can default
