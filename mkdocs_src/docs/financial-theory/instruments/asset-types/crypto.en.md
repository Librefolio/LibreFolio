# <img src="../../../static/icons/asset-types/crypto.png" width="32" style="vertical-align: middle;" /> Crypto

**Cryptocurrencies** are digital assets that use cryptographic technology for secure transactions on decentralized blockchain networks. They operate independently of traditional banking systems.

## 📊 Key Concepts

### 🔐 Decentralization

Unlike stocks or bonds, cryptocurrencies have no central issuer. The value is determined purely by supply and demand across global exchanges.

### 📈 Market Capitalization

$$
\text{Market Cap} = \text{Current Price} \times \text{Circulating Supply}
$$

Market cap is the primary metric for comparing crypto assets. Bitcoin typically represents 40-60% of total crypto market capitalization.

### 🔄 Trading Pairs

Crypto assets trade in **pairs** (e.g., BTC/USD, ETH/BTC). LibreFolio tracks each crypto as a single asset with a reference currency, using [FX rates](../../../user/fx/index.md) for conversion.

### ⚡ Staking

Some proof-of-stake (PoS) cryptocurrencies allow holders to "stake" their tokens to earn rewards — functionally similar to [interest](../asset-events/interest.md) on bonds.

---

## ⚠️ Considerations

- **High volatility**: Daily swings of 5-10% are common
- **Regulatory risk**: Rules vary widely by jurisdiction and are evolving rapidly
- **Tax treatment**: Many countries treat crypto differently from traditional assets — see [Taxation](../../fundamentals/taxation.md)
- **24/7 markets**: Unlike stocks, crypto never stops trading
