# 🏦 Financial Instruments

This section covers the building blocks of any portfolio: the **instruments** you can hold, the **transactions** that move them, and the **events** that affect their value.

---

## 📖 What's Inside

### 📊 [Asset Types](asset-types/index.md)

The asset classes LibreFolio supports — from equities and ETFs to bonds, crypto, and real estate. Each type has specific pricing behaviors, dividend/interest handling, and tax implications.

### 💸 [Transaction Types](transaction-types/index.md)

Every financial operation that changes your portfolio: buying, selling, dividends received, fees paid, and more. Understanding transactions is critical for accurate P&L and tax reporting.

### 📅 [Asset Events](asset-events/index.md)

Corporate actions and scheduled occurrences that affect an asset **globally** — dividends declared, stock splits, price adjustments, interest payments, and maturity settlements. Events are distinct from transactions: they happen to the asset, not to your portfolio.
